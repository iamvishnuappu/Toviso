About Us

restaurant style

Hotel toviso is a creative interpretation of the classic Indo-American favourite, offering modern fare with a refined twist. Shareables, great cocktails, tasty local desserts, and fresh pressed juices from Greenhouse Juicery, round out a menu featuring classic diner favourites like burgers, milkshakes, omelettes, and great fries of course. This ingredient focused, neighborhood hot spot, is open from 6 am to 3 am daily, and for brunch on weekends.
